Tribute to A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

About A. P.J. Abdulm Kalam

A.P.J Abdul Kalam, the "Missile Man of India," Was born on October 15, 1931,in Rameswaram, tamil Nadu. He served as the 11th President of India
and was a renowned scientist, known for his work in India's space and missile programs.

Achievements

  • Devloped India's first satellite launch vehicle(SLv-III)
  • Led India's successful nuclear test in 1998
  • Wrote books like "Wings of Fire" and "India 2020"
  • Received the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award
